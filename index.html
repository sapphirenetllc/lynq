Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about LYNQFAST assistance services, fees, and what we provide.

Are you an official cable or internet service provider?

No. We are an independent third-party assistance platform. LYNQFAST provides informational guidance and support related to internet and cable services, but we are not an ISP and do not provide connectivity services directly.

Do you sell internet, cable, or streaming plans?

No. We provide guidance and assistance only. LYNQFAST does not sell subscriptions, plans, or services. Our role is to help you understand your options and navigate provider processes.

Do you charge for your services?

Yes. Our service fees are separate from provider charges. We charge for the time and expertise we provide in offering guidance and assistance. Contact us for specific service pricing information.

Will I still be billed by my service provider?

Yes. Provider billing remains unchanged. If you subscribe to internet or cable services from a provider, you will be billed directly by that provider. Our assistance fees are completely separate.

Do you need my account passwords or sensitive information?

No. We never request provider passwords or sensitive account credentials. We provide informational guidance only and do not need access to your provider accounts.

Which providers do you support?

We may reference multiple providers for guidance purposes only. We have no affiliation, partnership, or endorsement arrangement with any internet, cable, or streaming service provider. All provider names mentioned are for informational purposes.

Are your services refundable?

Yes. Refund requests can be submitted within 7 to 15 days of service purchase, depending on the type of assistance provided and whether assistance has already been delivered. Please review our Refund Policy page for full eligibility details.
